Valpo A to Z Redesign

For this project, the goal was to migrate the A to Z hub on valpo.edu's old template to their new modern design. This page was an alphabetical index for Valparaiso University to help faculty/students navigate easily through the website. From there, we wanted to make better use of the white space and filter out any links that no longer worked.
