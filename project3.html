Valpo Graduate Report Redesign

For this project, the goal was to migrate the Graduate Report site on valpo.edu's old template to their new modern design. The objective for the new page was to be less image/graph based and more focused on past student success stories. The team and I felt that graduate success was a better title from an SEO perspective, and we had our writers provide more copy on the pages as well.
